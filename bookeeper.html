THE BEEKEEPER

TRAILER.

Director:David Ayer

Producer:Bill Block, Jason Statham, Chris Long, Kurt Wimmer

Writer:Kurt Wimmer

Release Date:January 12, 2024

Runtime:2h15m

Synopsis:Retired schoolteacher Eloise Parker lives a quiet life in Massachusetts, renting out a barn to Adam Clay, a seemingly unassuming beekeeper. However, tragedy strikes when Eloise falls victim to an online phishing scam, losing over $2 million—most of which belongs to a charity she manages. Devastated, Eloise takes her own life. Adam Clay discovers Eloise’s body and is promptly arrested by FBI agent Verona Parker, Eloise’s daughter. Although Eloise’s death is ruled a suicide, Clay is released. Verona reveals that the group responsible for robbing Eloise has been on the FBI’s radar but is challenging to track down.
